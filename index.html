Privacy Policy for FMFR | Mass Friends Remover 2K24

Effective Date: Saturday, 24 August 2024

Introduction

FMFR | Mass Friends Remover 2K24 is a browser extension designed to help you manage and remove unwanted friends on Facebook. This privacy policy outlines how we handle and protect your data when you use our extension.

Data Collection

Our extension collects the following data from Facebook temporarily:

  • Name: Names associated with your Facebook friends.
  • Image: Profile images of your Facebook friends.
  • Text: Any relevant text data associated with your Facebook friends.

This data is used solely to facilitate the following functionalities of the extension:

  • Selecting Individual Friends
  • Searching Friends
  • Selecting All Loaded Friends
  • Performing Mass Removal of Friends

Data Usage

Temporary Storage: The collected data is stored only for the duration of the current session. Once the page is reloaded, the data is automatically deleted and is no longer accessible.

User Actions: Data is utilized to perform actions such as selecting and removing unwanted friends from your Facebook account.

Data Security

Access Control: Only you, the user, have access to the data through the extension. The data cannot be accessed by anyone else, including the developers of the extension.

Privacy Assurance: We do not store, share, or sell any data. Your data remains private and is exclusively used to provide the extension's functionality.

Data Deletion

Automatic Deletion: All collected data is deleted when the page is refreshed. No data persists beyond the current session.

Data Protection

Security Measures: We employ strong security measures to protect your data and ensure its privacy. The extension is designed to prioritize your data security, and we have no access to the data you manage with our tool.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other reasons related to operational, legal, or regulatory needs. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
